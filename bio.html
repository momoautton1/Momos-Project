But when people list out all their accomplishments in their bios, they risk sounding a little egotistical. Sure, you might impress a handful of people with all those laurels, but many people who read your bio will end up feeling either intimidated or annoyed. Think about it: Is that how you want the majority of your readers to feel when they read your bio? To minimize the egoism that comes with talking about yourself, think about how you can list out your accomplishments without sounding like you're bragging.
